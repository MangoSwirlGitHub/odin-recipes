Cake

Cute homemade single-layered vanilla cake.

Baking can seem like a daunting task especially when you can hear that it is easy to mess up or it is like chemistry. However, this simple recipe will walk you step by step on how to make a tasty little cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 3/4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/2 cup of milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Grease a 9-inch square cake pan
  3. Using a mixing bowl, mix the butter and sugar
  4. Add eggs one at a time and incorporate them before adding the next one
  5. Mix in vanilla
  6. Combine flour and baking powder in a different bowl
  7. Add flour and baking powder combination to the other bowl and mix well
  8. Add milk and mix until the batter is smooth
  9. Pour batter into the greased pan
  10. Bake for about 30 minutes or until the cake is not wet on the inside (this can be tested by poking it with a toothpick)
  11. Let cool
  12. Top with your favorite icing or toppings
