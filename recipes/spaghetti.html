Spaghetti

This simple, yet delicious, spaghetti and meatballs recipe is perfect for a quick dinner or meal prepping.

Due to the simplistic nature of the recipe, very few ingredients are needed and for those that are, you likely will already have them in your kitchen. The cooking time is also really short so it is great for when you are busy but still want a tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 jar of pasta sauce
  • 1/3 cup of bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup of onion, chopped
  • 1 pound of dry spaghetti noodles
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/4 cup of milk

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix the egg, bread crumbs, milk, onion, and ground beef
  3. After mixing well, shape into your preferred size meatballs
  4. Arrange the meatballs on a baking pan with space in between them
  5. Bake until the meatballs reach an internal temperature of 165 degrees (smaller meatballs will finish faster)
  6. While baking, boil water and place noodles into the boiling water to cook them
  7. While waiting for the water to boil and meatballs to cook, heat the sauce in a saucepan by simmering it and stirring occasionally
  8. Add cooked meatballs to sauce
  9. Drain pasta
  10. Combine pasta with sauce and meatballs
  11. Serve or store in a refrigerated air-tight container
