Chicken Noodle Soup

A yummy bowl of chicken noodle soup which is perfect as an appetizer or main dish.

This chicken noodle soup recipe will surely make you feel good whether you are sick or even if you are just craving soup. Not only is it tasty, but it is also pretty healthy. Can be eaten in a small portion as an appetizer or a larger portion as an entree.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup of chopped celery
  • 14 and 1/2 ounces of chicken broth
  • 14 and 1/2 ounces of vegetable broth
  • 1/2 pound of chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of egg noodles
  • 1 cup of sliced carrots
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1.2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a large pot on medium-low heat so it will not burn
  2. Add onion and celery, cook until tender
  3. Add both broths, chicken, noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper
  4. Bring to a boil while stirring
  5. Lower heat to simmer for 20 mins
  6. Serve now or store in a refrigerated air-tight container for later
